Main

Bart Rogiers

I am a research scientist and the R community lead at the Belgian Nuclear Research Centre. My research interests are currently focused around the introduction of probabilistic programming in radiological characterization, and leveraging procedural solid texture synthesis for porous media reconstruction. I furthermore really enjoy introducing people to the modern side of R, and help them adopt it as their main workhorse for more reproducible science.
