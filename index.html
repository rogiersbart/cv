Main

Bart Rogiers

I am a scientific researcher and R enthusiast at the Belgian Nuclear Research Centre (aka SCK CEN), and also have a seat in the council of the International Association of Hydrogeologists’ Belgian chapter.

My research interests are currently focused around the introduction of probabilistic programming in hydrogeology and radiological characterization, and leveraging procedural texture synthesis in the framework of porous media reconstruction.

I furthermore really enjoy introducing people to the modern side of R, and help them adopt it as their main workhorse for more reproducible science.
